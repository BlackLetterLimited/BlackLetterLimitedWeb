Our Mission

Where Legal Expertise Meets Modern Engineering

BlackLetter Limited was founded on a singular conviction: the legal institutions that underpin American justice deserve software built by people who understand them from the inside. Our founders have argued before federal courts, served overseas in uniform, and spent careers navigating the very systems our software is designed to serve.


We develop bespoke software for law firms, federal agencies, and legal-adjacent organizations — from case management platforms to document automation, compliance engines, and secure inter-agency workflow tools.

What We Build

Legal Software, Engineered for Impact

Case Management Systems

Comprehensive platforms for tracking, organizing, and advancing litigation across federal dockets, with real-time status dashboards and deadline automation.

Compliance & Regulatory Tools

Software that maps evolving federal regulations to operational workflows, reducing exposure and ensuring auditability across all agency touchpoints.

Document Automation

Intelligent document assembly for briefs, motions, contracts, and federal filings — reducing turnaround time and eliminating manual error.

Inter-Agency Workflow Platforms

Secure, role-based platforms that connect legal teams across federal departments, enabling structured collaboration, handoffs, and evidentiary chain-of-custody.

Client & Matter Portals
